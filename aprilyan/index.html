VOCALOID, or often referred as VOCALO is an electronic song synthesis software that simulates human voice developed from the singing synthesis technology of YAMAHA that is first released in the January of 2004. VOCALOID characters or the most commonly known VOCALOID characters are also referred as piapro characters developed from Crypton Future Media. Piapro characters are the vocal artists served for vocaloid who are also called vocaloids. Hatsune Miku is collectively known as the "Piapro characters" ,, along with Megurine Luka, Kagamine Len, Kagamine Rin, MEIKO, and KAITO.They are also the first entry in Crypton's "Character Vocal Series" code-named CV01.

VOCALOID,通常被称为术力口.是在2004年1月首次发布的YAMAHA歌唱合成技术的基础上开发的模拟人声的电子歌曲合成软件。VOCALOID角色中最常见的六位歌姬也被称为piapro角色们,由Crypton Future Media开发。Piapro角色是为民娜歌唱的歌手们,也被称为虚拟歌姬。初音未来等角色们被统称为“Piapro人物”,与巡音流歌，镜音连, 镜音铃, MEIKO和KAITO一起。他们也是Crypton代号为CV01的“人物声乐系列”的第一套歌手。

ボーカロイド(VOCALOID)は、2004年1月に初めて発表されたYAMAHA歌唱合成技術を基に開発された、人の声を模した電子歌曲合成ソフトである。ボーカロイドキャラの中で最も多い5人の歌姫はpiaproキャラ達とも呼ばれ、クリプトン・フューチャーメディアが開発した。ピプロキャラクターはミンナのために歌う歌手たちで、バーチャル歌姫とも呼ばれる。巡音ルカ、鏡音レン、鏡音すず、MEIKO、KAITOとともに、初音ミクなどのキャラクターたちは通称「パイプロペルソナ」と呼ばれる。初音ミクはCryptonのCV01と呼ばれる「キャラクターボイスシリーズ」の第1弾アーティストでもある。

If you are interested or already planning to attend any of the concerts you might need to check this website, it includes detailed information on tickets, locations, dates, seats and merchandises. If you are interested in the past series of fashion design you might want to check magicalmirai.com too, it also includes information on drafts and specific design details that are not usually accessible elsewhere, it also owns an collection of annual work which can be helpful if you are interested into creating fan-art.
